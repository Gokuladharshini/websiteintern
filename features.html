SCAN & DELIVER


Our Scan & Deliver services are resuming from selected sites. Not all collections will be accessible immediately as they are spread over 30 buildings, each of which must be prepared and made safe.


Scan & Deliver is an electronic document delivery service which enables you to obtain scans of book chapters or journal articles from the Bodleian Libraries' print collections

We are offering two scanning services:Offsite scan and Library scan

COMPUTER & WIFI


Bodleian Libraries PCs are equipped with a range of software including Microsoft Office 2016 (Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint), browsers (Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Internet Explorer and Microsoft Edge), Endnote, GIMP and iTunes.


To log in to a Bodleian Libraries PC, use your barcode number and your password. There is more information about library passwords on our Passwords page.

GETTING INVOLVED IN OUR WORK


The Bodleian Libraries are a destination for students, scholars and visitors from across the globe. They provide an essential resource for teaching and research as well as an extended programme of free events for the public.


There are many ways to get involved in the work of the Bodleian Libraries, whether you simply have two minutes to spend on one of our crowdsourcing projects or would like to make a contribution to our fundraising projects.

IMAGING SERVICES


Imaging Services is the primary provider of digital images, photographic prints and paper prints from microfilm within the Bodleian Libraries.The department also administers permissions to use the images in electronic and non-electronic formats.


The Bodleian Libraries own and retain the copyright on all reproductions produced at all times. This is separate from the original copyright of the material.
